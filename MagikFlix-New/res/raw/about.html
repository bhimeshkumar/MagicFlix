About MagikFlix

MagikFlix is a one-of-a-kind video app that’s just for kids!

MagikFlix scours the internet for the best kid-appropriate video content, curates and brings it to the user in a safe, simple and easy to use package.

MagikFlix' auto-play feature which creates an endless playlist targeted to each user’s interests and age. Children from ages 3 to 12 can enjoy videos in categories ranging from Music to News to Science - all without ever having to leave the app!
